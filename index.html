Collected Dance Project

An asynchronous dance film

We usually see dance in one time and place. But dance can be more powerful when it connects performers across time and space. Using the filmic medium, this collective performs one piece, at different times in different locations. The result is one cohesive story meant to unifies a more global human experience.

The Rules

  • Each dancer films the whole piece in one location
  • Wearing an outfit appropriate for that location
  • Using at least two angles (Front full body, stage right full body, front torso, stage right feet only)
